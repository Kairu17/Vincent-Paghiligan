Why it is
created?

People uses different pens depending on what he is doing, we change color depending what is is required. As a person who appreciate art, I noticed that artist uses different shades of color, some wants to draw the scenrey infront of them. Then the idea of creating a pen that would capture colors infront of them came to mind, this will allow for artist or any normal person to carry a single pen and draw what he wants with beautiful shades of colors.
