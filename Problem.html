Problem?

Companies have manufacture many colored pens and some consumers, especially artist wants to acquire a great amount of colored pen. People has also, somewhat used pens for writing, signing of documents or just by doodling. But have you ever encountered that you need or want to change the color of your pen and notice that the color you want is not available? This pen can change the ink color based on what color the camera can detect.
